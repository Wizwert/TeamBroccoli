What can you do with this app?

This app can be used to answer important questions related to childhood

food insecurity at school such as:

1. How can I make healthy eating enjoyable?

2. How do I eliminate social pressures that cause children receiving free lunch to skip meals?

3. How do I hold children and parents accountable for children's eating habits at school?

Data from this application can be used to inform parents, teachers, public entities, and researchers about children's eating habits during the school day - something that has been difficult to quantify in the past.
